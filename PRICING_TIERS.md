## Tier summary

### Starter (small businesses / solopreneurs)
- Suggested price: **BWP 250 / month (~USD 21)**
- Channels: **Facebook + Instagram** (Website Forms can be an add-on)
- Features:
  - Basic automation rules (keyword triggers, reply templates)
  - 1 agent, up to 5k interactions/month
  - Email support (business hours)
  - Basic analytics dashboard
- AI: AI suggestions disabled or very limited (suitable for cost-conscious users)

### Pro (growing teams)
- Suggested price: **BWP 1,500 / month (~USD 125)**
- Channels: Facebook + Instagram + Website Forms
- Features:
  - Advanced automations, multi-agent support (up to 5 agents)
  - Priority email + chat support
  - 50k interactions/month
  - Enhanced analytics and audit logs
- AI: AI suggestions available (capped monthly credits); auto-reply toggle per rule

### Enterprise (scale & compliance)
- Suggested price: **Custom (starting at BWP 6,000+ / month ~USD 500)**
- Channels: All included; WhatsApp available as an add-on / pilot
- Features:
  - SSO, dedicated onboarding, SLA, high-volume allowances
  - Dedicated account manager and enterprise support
  - Custom integrations and compliance features (data retention, export)
- AI: Higher AI quotas, options for on-prem or private model routing (negotiable)

## AI usage boundaries (high-level)
- AI is provided as an **assistive feature** (suggested replies, summary, classification) â€” it should not be used as the sole decision engine for critical operations unless explicitly configured and reviewed.
